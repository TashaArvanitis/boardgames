I've been playing tons of amazing games with family and friends during this time of plague. Here are my favorites (so far).

NOTE: using things for free on the internet is all fun and games until it means game designers and programmers aren't getting fairly compensated for their work. I recommend:

  1. Own these games in hard copy. This means you're paying the designer a fair price. But it still doesn't thank the people who implement the game somewhere on a website.
  2. Pay or donate to the web developers who put your favorite games online. Even just a $5-10 contribution helps.
  3. In addition to (1) and/or (2), send designers a thank you email! They'll certainly appreciate it.
